Hi 👋

This is the first post on this website.

I have wanted to create a personal space and share things consistently for a long time, but I kept postponing it, telling myself I wasn't ready. In the end, I decided to stop waiting.

## Why a personal website?

Platforms like Medium and Dev.to are great, but writing in your own space feels different. From design to content, everything belongs to you.

Another reason is that I want to build an archive I can look back on over time. I want to keep track of what I learn, what I struggle with, and how I solve things.

## What will I write about here?

I don't want to promise an overly ambitious publishing schedule, but I do want to write as consistently as I can. I will mainly focus on:

- Real-world problems I face with Next.js / React
- Frontend performance and user experience
- Small but effective practices that make coding easier
- Technical and product-oriented notes from my day-to-day work

## What did I use on the technical side?

I wanted to start with a simple and fast setup:

- **Next.js 15 (App Router)**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **MDX** (to write posts quickly and comfortably)

I chose MDX for blog content because it keeps the comfort of Markdown while still allowing JSX when needed.

## Closing thoughts

Thanks for reading this far.

This first post is more of a quick “hello” message. In the next posts, I'll share more technical and detailed content.
